Capital Gains Breakdown

ATO Rules Applied — FY 2025–26

  • 50% CGT discount for assets held longer than 12 months (individuals)
  • Cost-base method: FIFO (First In, First Out)
  • Staking & mining rewards = ordinary income at market value on receipt (ATO guidance)
  • Crypto-to-crypto swaps are CGT disposal events — s104-10 ITAA97
  • NFT disposals treated as CGT assets
  • Capital losses offset capital gains; net losses cannot offset other income
